Crockpot Split Pea and Ham Soup

Ingredients

  • • 1 large onion, chopped
  • • 4 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • • 1 (16-ounce) package dried green split peas, sorted and rinsed
  • • 1 large meaty ham bone or smoked ham hock
  • • 4 cups chicken stock
  • • 2 cups water
  • • 1 bay leaf
  • • 1/2 teaspoon marjoram leaves
  • • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a 4 or 5-quart slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on high for 2 hours, then turn the appliance to low and cook for another 4 to 6 hours or until peas are almost dissolved and vegetables are tender.
  3. Remove ham bone or hock and cut meat off the bone.
  4. Chop meat into small pieces and stir back into the soup. Discard bone.
  5. Remove bay leaf from soup and discard. Correct seasoning, stir soup well, and serve.
